# Market Research Reports

## Overview

Generate professional-grade market research reports (50+ pages) modeled after deliverables from top consulting firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Gartner, Forrester).

**Key Features:**
- 50+ pages with extensive visual content
- Multi-framework approach: Porter's Five Forces, PESTLE, SWOT, BCG Matrix, TAM/SAM/SOM
- Data-driven analysis with research integration
- LaTeX output with professional formatting

## When to Use

- Creating comprehensive market analysis for investment decisions
- Developing industry reports for strategic planning
- Analyzing competitive landscapes and market dynamics
- Conducting market sizing exercises (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Preparing due diligence materials for M&A activities
- Developing go-to-market strategy documentation

## Report Structure (50+ Pages)

### Front Matter (~5 pages)
- Cover Page with hero visualization
- Table of Contents
- Executive Summary (2-3 pages): Market snapshot, investment thesis, key findings, strategic recommendations

### Core Analysis (~35 pages)
1. **Market Overview & Definition** (4-5 pages) — ecosystem mapping, stakeholders, historical context
2. **Market Size & Growth** (6-8 pages) — TAM/SAM/SOM, historical growth, projections, regional breakdown
3. **Industry Drivers & Trends** (5-6 pages) — PESTLE analysis, macroeconomic factors, technology trends
4. **Competitive Landscape** (6-8 pages) — Porter's Five Forces, market share, competitive positioning matrix
5. **Customer Analysis & Segmentation** (4-5 pages) — segment definitions, buying behavior, value drivers
6. **Technology & Innovation** (4-5 pages) — current stack, emerging tech, R&D investment, patent landscape
7. **Regulatory & Policy** (3-4 pages) — regulatory framework, compliance requirements, upcoming changes
8. **Risk Analysis** (3-4 pages) — risk heatmap (probability vs impact), mitigation strategies

### Strategic Recommendations (~10 pages)
9. **Strategic Opportunities** (4-5 pages) — opportunity sizing, strategic options, prioritization
10. **Implementation Roadmap** (3-4 pages) — phased plan, milestones, resource requirements
11. **Investment Thesis & Financial Projections** (3-4 pages) — scenario analysis, return expectations

### Back Matter (~5 pages)
- Methodology & Data Sources
- Detailed Market Data Tables
- Company Profiles
- References/Bibliography

## Workflow

1. **Define Scope** — market definition, geographic boundaries, time horizon
2. **Research & Data Gathering** — use web search, industry reports, company filings
3. **Apply Analysis Frameworks** — TAM→SAM→SOM, Porter's, PESTLE, SWOT, competitive positioning
4. **Generate Visuals** — market growth charts, TAM/SAM/SOM diagrams, Porter's Five Forces, risk heatmaps
5. **Write Report** — section by section following structure guide
6. **Quality Review** — verify page count, data sources, visual completeness

## Quality Standards

| Dimension | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Data Currency | No older than 2 years |
| Sourcing | All statistics attributed |
| Validation | Cross-reference multiple sources |
| Writing | Active voice, specific numbers, no vague qualifiers |
| Visuals | 300 DPI minimum, colorblind-friendly, labeled |
| Actionability | Recommendations specific and implementable |
